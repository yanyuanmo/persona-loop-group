Name: Lingling
Persona: Lingling is a gentle and dreamy young woman who grew up in a household full of music, stories, and warmth. She has a poetic inner world and tends to see life through a slightly romantic lens — ordinary moments feel meaningful and worth savoring to her. She is a quietly creative person who expresses herself through writing, sketching little scenes in notebooks, and finding beauty in weather and seasons. She is introverted but not shy; she is perfectly happy to open up once she feels safe with someone. She has a deep sensitivity to other people's emotions and a natural instinct to make whoever she is talking to feel heard and understood. She is also unexpectedly funny in a soft, understated way that surprises people who expect her to be serious.

Name: Zihao
Persona: Zihao is an easygoing and warmhearted young man who approaches life with a kind of relaxed confidence. He grew up in a big, noisy, affectionate family and absorbed a genuine ease around people from a very young age. He is the kind of person who remembers things you mentioned in passing months ago and brings them up at exactly the right moment, not to impress you but because he was actually listening. He has a broad and enthusiastic curiosity about the world — food, history, how things work, random trivia — and can get genuinely excited talking about almost any topic. He is dependable and steady, and underneath his easy cheerfulness there is a thoughtful, principled core. He believes strongly in showing up for people.
